Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18-20 pepperoni slices
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500°F
  2. Roll out the dough
  3. Add the toppings
    • Pizza sauce
    • Mozzarella cheese
    • Pepperoni
  4. Cook the pizza
  5. Slice and serve
